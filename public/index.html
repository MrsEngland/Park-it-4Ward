ABOUT PARK IT 4WARD


Park It 4Ward is a commmunity driven, shared parking project. We aim to be the digital version of the traditional parking meter, by allowing our users to share the cost of a parking spot if you don't need it for the full paid time. If you are looking for a spot click on the park button and see if any of our users have shared a vacant spot. If no spots are available, please Park It 4Ward so that another user can take advantage of your generosity, just as you will benefit from someone's in the future! Once you leave please click the leave button to let others know the spot is available.

*Please note all information is only as accurate as the user who inputs it, Park It 4Ward assumes no liability for fees incurred from use of inaccurate information.
